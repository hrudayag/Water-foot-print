Ways to Reduce Your Water Footprint

  • Take shorter showers.
  • Fix leaks in your home to prevent wastage.
  • Turn off the tap while brushing your teeth.
  • Choose water-efficient appliances.
  • Reduce meat consumption as livestock requires vast amounts of water.
